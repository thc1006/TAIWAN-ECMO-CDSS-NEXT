MIMIC-IV 3.1 Data Catalog
=========================

Source: physionet.org/files/mimiciv/3.1/

## Hospital (hosp/) Data:
- admissions.csv.gz - Hospital admissions
- patients.csv.gz - Patient demographics
- diagnoses_icd.csv.gz - ICD diagnosis codes
- procedures_icd.csv.gz - ICD procedure codes
- d_icd_diagnoses.csv.gz - ICD diagnosis dictionary
- d_icd_procedures.csv.gz - ICD procedure dictionary
- labevents.csv.gz - Laboratory events
- d_labitems.csv.gz - Laboratory item dictionary
- prescriptions.csv.gz - Medication prescriptions
- microbiologyevents.csv.gz - Microbiology cultures
- drgcodes.csv.gz - DRG codes

## ICU Data:
- chartevents.csv.gz (3.3GB) - Vital signs and flowsheet data
- d_items.csv.gz - Item dictionary for chartevents
- icustays.csv.gz - ICU stay information
- datetimeevents.csv.gz - Datetime events
- inputevents.csv.gz - Fluid inputs
- outputevents.csv.gz - Outputs (urine, drains)
- procedureevents.csv.gz - ICU procedures

Total Size: ~10GB compressed
