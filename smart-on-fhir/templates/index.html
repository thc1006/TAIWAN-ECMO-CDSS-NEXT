Clinical Decision Support System for ECMO Candidacy Assessment

About This Application

This SMART on FHIR application integrates with Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems to provide real-time ECMO candidacy assessment and risk stratification for critically ill patients. It uses NIRS monitoring, laboratory values, and clinical parameters to predict patient outcomes.
